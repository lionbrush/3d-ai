Experiment 1: Orb Animation

Overview

Hi Everybody! I recently rendered an animation of an orb with particles moving around inside. It seemed like a great video for experimenting with various AI tools. Here's a summary of my journey and findings.

Runway Results

I started by testing the video in Runway, trying different materials to see what results it could generate. However, some of the outputs were far from ideal, and unfortunately, I ran out of credits before being able to test further. Still, I plan to revisit it for improved results if I can.

Key Takeaways

1. Still images yielded more consistent, high-quality results than video-to-video transformations.
2. Slight adjustments to prompts can significantly influence the output.
3. Using 3D animations as AI bases provides unique starting points for experiments.

Final Thoughts

The rapid pace of AI model improvements is exhilarating but also costly. Subscriptions add up, and new tools appear almost daily. This project has been a fun (and sometimes frustrating) dive into the potential of AI in animation!
